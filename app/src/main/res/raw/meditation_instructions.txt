# Sample Meditation Music Files

To add custom music to your meditation app:

1. Download MP3 files from royalty-free sources:
   - Freesound.org
   - Pixabay Music
   - YouTube Audio Library
   - Zapsplat

2. Place files in: app/src/main/res/raw/
   - meditation_1.mp3
   - meditation_2.mp3
   - meditation_3.mp3
   - custom_sound.mp3

3. Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG

4. File size limit: Keep under 5MB for better performance

5. Recommended length: 3-10 minutes for meditation loops
