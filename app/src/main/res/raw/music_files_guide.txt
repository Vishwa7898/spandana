# Custom Meditation Music Files

Place these MP3 files in: app/src/main/res/raw/

## Required Files:
1. bell_meditation.mp3 - Bell meditation sounds
2. ocean_waves.mp3 - Ocean waves sound
3. nature_sounds.mp3 - Nature sounds (birds, rain, etc.)
4. ambient_music.mp3 - Ambient meditation music

## File Requirements:
- Format: MP3, WAV, or OGG
- Size: Under 5MB each
- Length: 3-10 minutes (will loop)
- Quality: 128kbps or higher

## Download Sources:
- Freesound.org (free)
- Pixabay Music (free)
- YouTube Audio Library (free)
- Zapsplat (free)

## Recommended Sounds:
- Bell meditation chimes
- Ocean wave sounds
- Forest nature sounds
- Ambient meditation music
